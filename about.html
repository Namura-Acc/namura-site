About

An indie workshop for practical software

Namura is a non-commercial indie lab exploring simple tools for people who work with records, checklists, field notes, admin workflows, and documents.

Built small on purpose

Namura is not trying to look like a large software company. It is closer to a workshop: small experiments, practical prototypes, and tools that solve narrow problems without extra ceremony.

The goal is to make useful things that respect attention, keep records clear, and help people move through repetitive tasks with less friction.

Working principles

  • Use plain interfaces.
  • Keep data collection minimal.
  • Prefer readable outputs.
  • Let users verify and edit results.
  • Build for everyday workflows.

Scope

What Namura explores

Productivity

Small helpers for formatting, tracking, and getting routine work into shape.

Field operations

Checklist and monitoring concepts for tasks that happen outside a desk workflow.

Documents

Utilities for records, minutes, registers, notices, and review-friendly outputs.

Automation

Small experiments that reduce repeated steps while keeping human review in the loop.
