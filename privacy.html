Minimal data approach

Namura tools are designed to collect as little data as possible. Where a tool can run locally or without an account, that approach is preferred.

Website information

This static website does not include analytics scripts, advertising trackers, or third-party client-side dependencies. Hosting providers may still process basic technical information, such as IP addresses and request logs, as part of normal website delivery.

Individual app notices

Individual Namura apps may have their own privacy notices. Those notices should be read alongside this general policy because each tool may handle data differently.

Contact
