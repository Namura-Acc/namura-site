FAQ

Common questions

Is Namura a company?

No. Namura is presented as a non-commercial indie lab and software workshop.

Are the tools commercial?

No. The tools are described as non-commercial experiments, concepts, or utilities unless a specific project page says otherwise.

How do I report a bug?

Email the support address with the tool name, what happened, what you expected, and any steps needed to reproduce the issue.

Do the tools collect data?

Namura tools are designed to collect as little data as possible. Individual apps may include their own privacy notices with more specific details.
